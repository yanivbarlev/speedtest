About speedtst.online

Welcome to speedtst.online, your free and reliable internet speed testing tool. We're dedicated to helping you measure and understand your internet connection speed with accuracy and ease.

What We Do

speedtst.online provides a simple, fast, and accurate way to test your internet connection speed. Whether you're troubleshooting slow internet, verifying your ISP's promised speeds, or just curious about your connection performance, we've got you covered.

How Our Speed Test Works

Our speed test measures three key metrics of your internet connection:

  • Download Speed - How fast you can receive data from the internet (measured in Mbps)
  • Upload Speed - How fast you can send data to the internet (measured in Mbps)
  • Ping (Latency) - How long it takes for data to travel to a server and back (measured in milliseconds)

Understanding Your Results

After running a speed test, you'll see three main results:

  • Download Speed - This is typically the most important metric for most users. It affects how quickly web pages load, videos stream, and files download.
  • Upload Speed - Important for video calls, uploading files to cloud storage, and live streaming.
  • Ping - Lower is better. Important for online gaming and video conferencing. Under 50ms is excellent, 50-100ms is good.

When to Test Your Speed

We recommend testing your internet speed:

  • When experiencing slow internet or buffering videos
  • After upgrading your internet plan to verify you're getting what you pay for
  • When setting up a new router or modem
  • At different times of day to see if your speeds vary
  • Before and after troubleshooting connection issues

Tips for Accurate Results

  • Connect your device directly to the router with an Ethernet cable if possible
  • Close all other applications and browser tabs
  • Pause any downloads, uploads, or streaming
  • Test multiple times at different times of day
  • Make sure no one else is using the internet during the test
