Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

At speedtst.online, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our speed testing service.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our speed test, we automatically collect:

  • IP Address - Your public IP address to determine your approximate location and ISP
  • Speed Test Results - Download speed, upload speed, and ping measurements
  • Connection Information - Your ISP name, city, and country (derived from your IP)
  • Browser Information - Browser type and version for compatibility purposes
  • Device Information - Device type (mobile, tablet, or desktop)

Information Stored Locally

We store your test history locally in your browser using localStorage. This includes:

  • Your last 20 speed test results
  • Timestamps of when tests were run
  • Your preferences (Multi/Single connection mode)

Important: This data is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers. You can clear this data at any time using the "Clear History" button or by clearing your browser data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Perform accurate speed tests and display your results
  • Show you your ISP and location information
  • Improve our service and fix technical issues
  • Understand usage patterns to enhance user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

IP Geolocation Services

  • ipapi.co - To determine your location and ISP from your IP address
  • ipify.org - Backup service to detect your IP address if the primary service fails

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • ipapi.co Privacy Policy: https://ipapi.co/privacy/

Hosting Services

  • Vercel - Our website is hosted on Vercel's infrastructure

4. Cookies and Tracking

speedtst.online does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. We only use browser localStorage to store your test history locally on your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Track you across other websites

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • We implement security headers to prevent common web vulnerabilities
  • We don't store sensitive personal information on our servers
  • Test history is stored locally on your device, not on our servers

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - View your test history stored locally in your browser
  • Delete - Clear your test history at any time using the "Clear History" button
  • Opt-Out - Stop using our service at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: Contact form on our contact page
  • Website: speedtst.online

11. International Users

speedtst.online is operated globally. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.
